About Talent Boon

Our motto ”Inspiring the skillful” aims at promoting the artistic craftwork,stylish garments and and the authentic food flavors made by talented makers from different parts of Kerala. We also aim at making a cellular network of social interface with people of same district through our blog and community section.

Vision

The unit is presently set up for Ernakulam district. We started this startup in 2017 to provide opportunity to showcase talent and also to provide means to earn income from this small effort by us.Our mission is only development and progress of our nation and this is achievable by the development of remote places. Along with these products we also help people to stay healthy and fit by providing fresh organic vegetables and fruits. There is a separate service Centre (community section) here the customers can contact to our skilled and well experienced makers. Here food section involves the meal, special dishes, festival special, snacks and dessert foodstuff from ‘God’s own country’. Customers can place their order on the day and date they prefer. Customer can book our special varieties for birthday events, marriage functions and for festival seasons.Self help groups like-kuttukari, swarna and swapnam provides even catering services.Our small effort will make the core of cooking now easier for the bachelors and working women.No struggle for food. We help our users to contact these people.Even individual makers get opportunity to sell their products. People can place their order i.e the food they want by contacting our individual makers. Art gallery section contains paintings ,sculptures ,handbags ,mats and other household materials .People can buy there desired list of products. These are purely handmade beautiful items which is now available at low price and easily available .Now you can find the special and unique home decors here at ‘Talent Boon’.Choose your dress, jewellery trendy sandals and bags from ethnic varieties we provide.Now you can dress like the fashion divas you want. Just on one click you can dress as you want.Your wedding gowns or stylish kurtis is ready at your wish one call and our designers will design and stich your dress as per your wish.You can either buy the dresses we provide or can also change and modify the wardrobe. Even you can contact the designer through our community section.Our trained tailors and designers will stich your wardrobe.You can clear your doubts and product related details through our call center.

Nowadays we all are familiar with the fertilized and pesticide treated vegetables and fruits. Here our talented agriculture lovers provide you the fresh and pesticide free vegetables and fruits. Now your health is safe in our hands. The community section not only provides the easy access to all common services .Our services include stiching, designing, plumbing ,cleaning and maintenance ,painting,transportation, electric works, fitting and fixing, rubber and woodcutters, blacksmith, goldsmith and Mehendi designers.Even you can get the details about any hospitals nearby grocery shop through our site.We also provide a blood bank section for the users here people can get the blood or request for any rare blood group to our daily users through our blog section. Our blog section provides users the opportunity to share their views and suggestions. You can also share your views and opinions about any social issue.We here not only promote the talented people but also provide the platform for socializing. A district based socializing is seen which makes us unique. We uplift people makes life easier for everyone. We believe in sustainable life so our aim is using the natural and mostly available products as far as possible. Creating no harmful and chemical products as far as possible.We also promote tourism and major attractions in area where we function.News and updates of our current unit is present.Daily bonus,surprises and discounts during festive season is provided.
